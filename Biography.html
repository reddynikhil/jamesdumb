Who is the real Schneider?

Rob was born and raised in San Francisco, California, in 1963. He first began stand-up whilst at high school, but his first notable comedy act was in 1988 when he was signed on to be a regular on Saturday Night Live. Since then Rob has starred in a number of notable films, such as "Deuce Bigalow: Male Gigolo (1999)" and "Deuce Bigalow: European Gigolo (2005)", as well as working alongside noteworthy actors like David Spade and Patricia Schneider.

Achievements

  • Nominated for a Blockbuster Entertainment Award in the "Favourite Actor - Comedy" category for Deuce Bigalow: Male Gigolo - 2000
  • Nominated for an MTV Movie Award in the "Sexiest Performance" category for Deuce Bigalow: European Gigolo - 2006
  • Nominated for a Razzie Award in the "Worst Actor" category for Deuce Bigalow: European Gigolo - 2006

What's next for Rob?

Rob is currently working on his sitcom entitled "Real Rob (2016-present)" where he plays the an idealised version of himself. He is also in an upcoming film entitled Sandy Wexler, where he will be working alongside Kevin James and Adam Sandler. This is the second movie the three have worked together on, with the first being the 2010 Buddy film/Comedy "Grown-ups".
